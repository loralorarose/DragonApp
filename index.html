Dragon types

Fire Dragons

As the name suggests, fire dragons have everything to do with fire.

1. they can breathe fire any time they want, with no recharge required.
2. they are resistant to any kind of flame or lava.
3. they are very good flyers, but they are deathly afraid of water or ice.

Some fire dragon sub-types include lava dragons and magma dragons.

Sea Dragons

Sea dragons have these common abilities.

1. they can breathe under water and on land
2. they are exremely fast swimmers, however they are slow fliers and cannot dig
3. they take no damage from sea dragon attacks, but fire dragons' attacks do 1 and a half times the normal amount

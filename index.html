Dragonlife

This is an experimental dragon game. You, the player, are a dragon trainer. You have just gotten your first dragon, and your job is to train it. You will encounter other wild dragons on the way, and you will be able to train them. It wont be easy though. When your dragon levels up, it will get better abilities. veiw the dragon level up sheet HERE .

Here are be some starter dragons that I will be showcasing, each with an explanation.

Fire Dragons

A cute pet to flare up imagination!

Fire Dragons can breathe fire any time
they want, with no recharge required.
They are resistant to any kind of flame or lava
and they are good flyers. Although,
They are deathly afraid of water or ice.

Water Dragons

A companion, always there for you.

Water Dragons can breathe under water and
on land. They are great swimmers,
but slow flyers and they cannot dig.
They take no damage from fellow sea dragons,
but fire dragons do twice as much.
